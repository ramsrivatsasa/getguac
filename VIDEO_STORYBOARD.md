# GetGuac Explainer Video — Storyboard

> **Important upfront:** I (Claude) can write the script, scene-by-scene direction,
> on-screen text, and motion-graphics specs — but I can't render the actual video.
> Once you approve the storyboard, you'd produce it in one of these tools (in order of cost/quality):
>
> - **Free:** [Canva Video](https://www.canva.com/video-editor/) — drag-drop, has avocado-friendly templates
> - **Free, AI-driven:** [Vidnoz](https://vidnoz.com/), [Pictory](https://pictory.ai) — turns a script into a video
> - **Cheap pro-grade:** [Descript](https://descript.com) (~$15/mo) — AI voice + screen recordings
> - **Hire a freelancer:** Fiverr "explainer video" gigs, ~$150-400 for a 90s animated piece
>
> Show them this doc, and they can ship it in a day or two.

---

## Target length
**90 seconds** — long enough to land the story, short enough that people actually watch it.

Aspect ratios to export:
- 16:9 (YouTube, landing page hero)
- 9:16 (TikTok, Instagram Reels, mobile app store preview)
- 1:1 (Twitter/X, LinkedIn auto-play)

## Tone
- Warm, smart, slightly cheeky — the avocado mascot is the host
- Music: upbeat lo-fi, ~110 BPM, ducks under voice-over
- Voice-over: friendly first-person ("Hi, I'm Guac…"), conversational, not corporate

## Brand palette (use throughout)
- Emerald-700 `#15803d` — primary
- Lime-300 `#a3e635` — pop
- Amber-500 `#f59e0b` — receipts/AI accent
- Rose-500 `#e11d48` — "bank bite" warnings
- Slate-50 / white — backgrounds

# Scene-by-scene

## SCENE 1 — Hook (0:00–0:08)
**Visual:** Avocado mascot (`celebrating` expression) bounces in on white background. Cluttered desk behind it covered in paper receipts.

**VO:** "Your receipts are everywhere. Your spending insights are nowhere. Sound familiar?"

**On-screen text:** `Where did all my money go? 🤔`

## SCENE 2 — Meet Guac-AI (0:08–0:18)
**Visual:** Receipts vacuum up into the mascot, who morphs slightly into a tiny floating brain (`thumbsup` expression).

**VO:** "Meet GetGuac — a Guac-AI personal finance assistant that reads your receipts, scores your spending, and keeps your money's bite out of your bank."

**On-screen text:** `🥑 GetGuac · Your money's wingman`

## SCENE 3 — The two-email magic (0:18–0:38) — **THIS IS THE BIG ONE**
**Visual:** Split screen.
  - Left: a personal-looking email envelope, label `you@getguac.app`, with a 🔒 padlock and big text `PRIVATE`.
  - Right: a glowing magic envelope, label `you+receipts@getguac.app`, with a sparkle ✨ and big text `AUTO-PROCESS`.

Arrows fly in from the left side showing examples (Amazon, Walmart, Spotify icons) landing in the LEFT mailbox. Then a single email is "forwarded" with a curved arrow from left to right — and on the right side, the AI brain pulls it apart into a clean receipt card.

**VO:** "Every account comes with two free email addresses. Use the first to sign up for Amazon, Walmart, your loyalty programs — and that mailbox stays private. We never read it."

(Beat 1s)

"Forward only the receipts you want filed to your magic `+receipts` address. That's the only thing Guac-AI ever touches."

**On-screen text:** appears progressively:
- `PERSONAL ←  you@getguac.app  ·  Guac-AI never reads`
- `OPT-IN  →  you+receipts@getguac.app  ·  Auto-parsed in 10 min`

## SCENE 4 — Privacy promise (0:38–0:50)
**Visual:** The mascot in `angel` expression (the brand/logo pose), wings out, halo glowing. Below it, three line items with checkmarks fade in.

**VO:** "Your personal mail is yours. We never poll it. We can't see other users' data — row-level encryption stops that at the database. And one tap wipes everything."

**On-screen text (animated checklist):**
- ✅ Personal inbox: never read
- ✅ Receipts hook: 100% opt-in
- ✅ One-click account + data delete

## SCENE 5 — What the AI gives back (0:50–1:10)
**Visual:** Split into 3 vertical panels showing animated screenshots:
1. **GuacScore** — a big "87" with a circular ring filling green.
2. **GuacWizard** — a wand emoji + "Hidden fees: $42 this month" with a 🦷 emoji.
3. **Stash** — a grid of repeat-purchase items with little arrows ("Time to restock!")

**VO:** "What you get back is a real CFO in your pocket. A GuacScore for every dollar you've spent. The GuacWizard hunts down hidden bank fees. The Stash predicts when you'll run out of your favorites."

**On-screen text:** `📊 Score · 🧙‍♂️ Wizard · 📦 Stash · 💎 Steals · 🦷 Bite Tracker`

## SCENE 6 — Mobile + Web (1:10–1:20)
**Visual:** Phone and laptop side by side. Both show the dashboard. Synchronized animation showing data syncing between them with little dotted lines.

**VO:** "On your phone. On your laptop. Same brain, same data, available wherever you are."

**On-screen text:** `📱 Android app · 🌐 Web app · 🔄 Same Guac-AI`

## SCENE 7 — Call to action (1:20–1:30)
**Visual:** Mascot (`rich` expression — sunglasses + cash) holds up a sign reading **getguac.app**. Email handle generator animates through `@you`, `@sara`, `@dave`, `@yours`.

**VO:** "Free forever. No card. No spam. No ads. Just your guac, smashed daily. Get yours at GetGuac.app."

**On-screen text:** `🥑 getguac.app · Claim @yourname today`

End card holds for 3 seconds: GetGuac logo + URL.

# Production budget estimate

| Path | Cost | Time |
|---|---|---|
| DIY in Canva Video | $0 | 4-8 hrs |

| Descript with custom voice | $15/mo | 3-4 hrs |
| Fiverr freelancer | $150-400 | 2-5 days |
| Studio + animator | $1500+ | 1-2 weeks |

If launching in days not weeks, **AI-driven + a small Canva polish pass** is the right tradeoff.
